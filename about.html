About

After completing my adult requirements of working a 9-5, raising a child and keeping house, it's finally time to breath life into all the fantastical ideas I've had all my life. I have an Art degree and I'm not afraid to use it! â˜º

I love all things of nature, and would much rather paint a tree or any animal than man-made objects. Though while sketching that tree, my mind is busy populating it with elves, fairies, and dragons. Sometimes my imaginings are a little dark, but don't be afraid! I see beauty all around me, and I try not to judge whatever others may find beautiful as well.

I love all mediums equally, though some are more challenging than others, but my true love is exploring how I can make use of or incorporate modern tools, supplies and techniques to create something new. I don't like to do just one thing over and over, even if I do it well, so while I can make something that's similar to a particular work, I will generally move on from subject to subject, so if you spot something you like, it's best to grab it right away. That said, I will be happy to discuss revisiting a subject or style or personalized works.

My subject matter covers conventional representational to abstract and everything in between so come explore the world with me because while adulting is important, so is our inner landscape. I appreciate any feedback you have for me and hope you enjoy my work.
